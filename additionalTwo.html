Coates

A guide on Coates enclosures

Coates screens are always 55” Samsung Outdoor screens, which we receive either through Atos, already ordered under a Case C1234567, or we order them through the Samsung B2B portal, if in-warranty.
If out of warranty, Atos incident is sent to Coates for quote and remediate. (Physical damage issues are considered out of warranty also).

After the Display has been replaced, a pick-up is requested through the Samsung B2B portal, and Atos & Snow tickets are updated on that matter, and closed.
All Coates in-warranty hardware should be returned to the warehouse through a pick-up process done after the replacement is completed.
Regarding enclosure issues, and Eyebolt issue (which is usually sent to us through a Sales request), Coates is the one handling that.
To request a pick-up, after searching with the case number, and locating the screen, in the right side of that request, there is a field called “pick-up request” where a pick-up date can be entered.

To order a new screen, click on New, and select the following:
Business name: Main BP McDonalds
Store: (store number)
Case type: FT, FT/WG
After that, press Create, and a Samsung Case will be created.
In it, at the bottom right side, where will be an option to order multiple screens.
At the product information, press New button, and enter the following:
Serial #: (More on this below)
Symptoms: (Select appropriate)
Attachment: (Add a picture of the screen)
Problem Description: (Root cause of the problem) In the Serial number field, after pressing Check, most of the things will be auto-filled, along with the warranty expiration date, so this is a good way of checking the warranty of the equipment.
If any equipment is not picked up, even after requesting a pick-up, escalate with an email to USSI, and add Case#, Ticket#, Serial#, Store# If any equipment has not been shipped, escalate to SamsungB2BSupport with an email, and request an update.

Stratacache

A guide on Stratacache enclosures

Stratacache Displays are either indoor 49” LG screens, or outdoor 55” LG(old) or AUO(new) Displays, where LG and AUO screens should not be in the same enclosure, as per Stratacache.
The equipment is most usually processed as an out-of-warranty request that we send to the Sales team for a quote.
There are in-warranty request, which are rare, but they come through an Atos ticket.
All of the Stratacache hardware is replaced by NCR. For a pick-up, a return label should come, along with the boxed hardware.
If not, the customer would need to obtain it through Stratacache.

For an out-of-warranty request, the hardware does not need to be returned, and can be disposed of by the store, according to the local laws/rules on hardware disposal.
Stratacache enclosures are opened by two set of keys, and the enclosure contains multiple different parts, which can all be replaced by NCR.
