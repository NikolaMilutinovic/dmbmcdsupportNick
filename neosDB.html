DMB L2 Support handles multiple types of visits, B&F visits for standard technicians, B&F for smaller installations, Deployment tasks for larger Scope of Work, and Cabling visits.
B&F visits are done through Neos and Dispatchboard, and its designed for troubleshooting visits, single-screen replacement, Media Player replacements, hdmi/rs232/psu replacement.

Neos/Dispatchboard

A guide on Neos Incident creation

To create a ticket, we would first need to open a Neos Incident.
To open one, in Neos, select Smart recorder tab, and in the newly opened tab, in the field thats now opened for population, add the stores FUA number with the format “@FUA” and select the store that is now opened. The stores FUA number can be obtained through a Master Restaurant List.
Once the store is selected, change the name of the subject to “NSN (store number) / ODMB Display 11 troubleshooting” or whatever the scope of work should be.
Scroll down and select the appropriate template, here are the most commonly used:
1666-7165-8801 / standard tech resource => Standard technician for t/s & mp/cable replacement
1666-7173-8801 / 2 to 43-55" Outdoor DMB => Two technicians for screen replacement
Once that is selected, press the “Create Ticket” button, and select Create incident.

A new page will be opened, where we need to change “Status” at the top of the page to In Progress, and then scroll down to the middle of the page, where we change “SN Override” to No, and Serial number to “N/A”, and keep scrolling to the part where it says “Assigned to: None set”. Press Assign to me, and in the right part of screen, a pop-up will show up, where we should select our name, with the NCR Corporations > Support > DMB SUPPORT tag, and then press Assign.
After Neos ticket is created, scroll all the way down to Tasks, and select “Add task” .
In the new Pop-up, select HSR Dispatch and Done. Open the new task, press “Edit” in the top-right corner.
Change the Status to Assigned, and then scroll down to Failure Reason, where we enter “%%%”, which will allow us to select No reason entered. OOS should be Yes if the customer will be charged, No if the customer shouldnt be charged, and OOS Reason “T&M charge” if they are being charged, and “N/A” if not.
Last step in Neos is to select Submit HSR Dispatch, and select Yes.

Now, a new Dispatch will be created in the Dispatchboard, it can be located through the “Site Name” row, which should contain the NSN of the site.
In the dispatch, press “Take Ownership” and go to Tech & FSL tab, where we will select NCR CE, and circle the assign button.
Once that is done, go back to Work Order Info tab, and enter the desired ETA of the installation in the Tech ETA grey field. Then, open Work Order Notes, and select “Create Note”, where we will add a template containing ETA,SOW etc.
Once that is completed, press Save, and go to New NCR dispatch, where we will change the bottom “Initial remarks” field, with a template for a technician. Once that is completed, press Send to NCR. Then, press cancel, and check what the “View Work Order” number is in the title, and check for an EPS email to get sent to your team, containing that work order number.
Send an email to the appropriate field nation, edit the template, and proceed with informing the customer.

Quickbase/PSV

A guide on PSV Incident creation

For larger visits, and also cabling visits, PSVs are created. To create them, we operate through the QuickBase and select Project Sites.
In the “Search these Project Site records”, type the store number, and enter.
Select “McDonalds DMB MAC” option, and locate the store by the CDM Sites / Site Number row. When located, press the Eye icon, if the store is not on the list, press the blue button “New Project Site”, and enter 746 in the first field, and the stores FUA in the second field. Once the stores Project Site is opened, scroll down to Add Task, and select it.

Populate Related Template Task with “L2 Cabling” option or “Replace Outdoor Display / Stratacache”, scroll down and populate “First Scheduled Date, Last Scheduled Date, Earliest Start Time, Latest Start Time”.
Then, scroll down and populate “Work Order Notes Override” with the template for PSVs, and press save. Then, after Task is created, send a message to the McD DMB Phase 2 group, stating “NSN (store number) - PSVs created”.
Then, track the ETA through the next couple of days.
